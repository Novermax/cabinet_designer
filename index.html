Powerful Features

Everything You Need
to Build Beautiful Cabinets

Designed for professional woodworkers and hobbyists alike.

Parametric 2D & 3D Design

Drive the whole cabinet from parameters. Resize, split openings, add dividers, doors and shelves with a live 3D preview.

  • Parametric openings & dividers
  • Real-time 3D preview
  • Undo / redo & project save

Photorealistic Rendering

Turn compositions into client-ready visuals. Real materials and lighting produce studio-quality room renders straight from your design.

  • Studio-quality room renders
  • Real materials & lighting
  • Share visuals with clients

Cabinet Typologies

Not just boxes. Design standard carcasses, diagonal corner units and slanted-top cabinets — each with its own parametric geometry.

  • Standard carcass
  • Diagonal corner unit (angoliera)
  • Slanted-top unit — 5-axis machining

Catalogs & Hardware Library

Editable material and edge-band palettes plus a full hardware library. Assign materials and hardware per panel, per door, per edge.

  • Editable material & edge-band catalogs
  • Hinges, guides, handles, feet, joints
  • Blum Tandem / Movento guides

Cut List & Optimization

Auto-generate detailed cut lists with grain direction and edge-band compensation, so the panel saw gets the real finished sizes.

  • Auto cut-list generation
  • Edge-band size compensation
  • Export to Excel / CSV

Native CNC Export — SCM Maestro

Export ready-to-run programs for SCM Maestro. Drilling, milling, blade cuts and toe-kick pockets emitted as native Maestro operations.

  • SCM Maestro post-processors (XCS)
  • Milling, blade cuts, toe-kick pockets
  • F6 bottom-face split & DXF / CSV
