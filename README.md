# Everything You Need to Build Beautiful Cabinets 🪚

> **Designed for professional woodworkers and hobbyists alike.**

---

## ✨ Powerful Features

### 📐 2D & 3D Design
Drag-and-drop cabinet assembly with real-time 3D preview. Adjust dimensions, materials, and hardware in seconds.
* **Parametric cabinet editor**
* **Real-time 3D rendering**
* **Material & hardware library**

### 📋 Cut List & Optimization
Auto-generate detailed cut lists with grain direction, edge-banding, and panel optimization to minimize waste.
* **Auto cut-list generation**
* **Nesting optimization**
* **Export to Excel / PDF**

### 🖥️ CNC Export
Directly export toolpaths for your CNC router. Supports G-code, STEP, and popular machine formats.
* **G-code & STEP export**
